Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't find the answer you need, please contact us!

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

No, many of our clients provide us with a spare key or garage code. Our team is fully insured, and your security is our priority.

What cleaning products do you use?

We primarily use **eco-friendly**, non-toxic, and biodegradable products to ensure safety for your family and pets. We can accommodate special requests if you provide the products.

How do I pay for the service?

We accept all major credit cards and bank transfers. Payment is processed after the service is completed.

What is your cancellation policy?

We request at least 24 hours notice for any cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours may incur a small fee.
